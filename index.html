About Us

We are a neighborhood restaurant blending classic flavors with contemporary technique. Our chefs craft seasonal dishes with locally sourced produce.

Founded in 2021, The borcelle restaurant is a cozy spot for family dinners, date nights, and weekend brunches.

Signature Menu

Chicken Tikka Masala

Chicken Tikka Masala is a well-known Indian dish that consists of marinated chicken pieces cooked in a flavourful, spiced tomato sauce with ingredients like capsicums, ginger, and onions.

Palak Paneer

Palak Paneer is one of the delicious vegetarian dishes that’s both healthy and full of flavour, with paneer cheese and a creamy spinach sauce as its main ingredients. The dish is seasoned with fresh ginger and other spices, offering a delectable flavour and a wealth of nutrients, making it perfect for stage catering events.

₹349
Masala Dosa

Masala Dosa is a thin, crispy crepe made from a combination of rice and lentils, filled with a spiced potato mixture and served with chutneys and sambar. Not only is this dish delectable, but it also packs numerous health benefits.

₹99
Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori Chicken is a popular Indian dish featuring chicken marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture and cooked in a traditional tandoor oven. The tandoor oven is integral to the preparation of Tandoori Chicken, as it provides a unique cooking atmosphere that imparts a smoky flavor to the dish and cooks the chicken rapidly and evenly.

₹449
Vegetable Biryani

Vegetable Biryani is a fragrant rice dish cooked with a variety of vegetables and spices, making it perfect for sharing.

₹299
Mango Lassi

Mango Lassi is a sweet and creamy mango-yogurt drink that is perfect for cooling down after a spicy meal. This refreshing beverage originates from the Punjab region of the Indian subcontinent.
